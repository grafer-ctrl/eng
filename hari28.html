📖 PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS

Digunakan untuk: aktivitas yang sudah dimulai dan masih berlangsung sampai sekarang.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + have/has + been + V-ing + O

(-) S + have/has + not + been + V-ing + O

(?) Have/Has + S + been + V-ing + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) She has been waiting for an hour.

Dia sudah menunggu selama satu jam.


(-) She has not been waiting for an hour.

Dia belum menunggu selama satu jam.


(?) Has she been waiting for an hour?

Apakah dia sudah menunggu selama satu jam?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

for, since, all day, recently, lately

2️⃣ Have / Has Been

I/You/We/They → have been

He/She/It → has been

3️⃣ Gunakan Verb + ING

study → studying
wait → waiting
play → playing

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Selalu pakai HAVE/HAS + BEEN

✅ Kata kerja harus tambah ING

✅ Dipakai untuk aktivitas yang masih berlangsung

✅ Kata kunci sering memakai FOR dan SINCE

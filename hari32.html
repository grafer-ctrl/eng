📖 PAST PERFECT CONTINUOUS

Digunakan untuk: aksi yang sudah berlangsung lama sebelum aksi lain di masa lalu.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + had + been + V-ing + O

(-) S + had + not + been + V-ing + O

(?) Had + S + been + V-ing + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) They had been waiting for 2 hours before the bus came.

Mereka sudah menunggu selama 2 jam sebelum bus datang.


(-) They had not been waiting for long.

Mereka belum lama menunggu.


(?) Had they been waiting for 2 hours?

Apakah mereka sudah menunggu selama 2 jam?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

for, since, before, all day

2️⃣ Selalu Pakai HAD BEEN

Semua subject:

I/You/We/They/He/She → HAD BEEN

3️⃣ Verb + ING

study → studying
wait → waiting
play → playing

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Semua subject pakai HAD BEEN

✅ Setelah BEEN harus verb + ING

✅ Dipakai untuk aksi yang sudah lama berlangsung

✅ Biasanya ada FOR atau SINCE

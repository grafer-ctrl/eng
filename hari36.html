📖 FUTURE PERFECT CONTINUOUS

Digunakan untuk: aksi yang akan sudah berlangsung selama waktu tertentu di masa depan.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + will + have + been + V-ing + O

(-) S + will + not + have + been + V-ing + O

(?) Will + S + have + been + V-ing + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) By next month, I will have been working here for 5 years.

Bulan depan, saya akan sudah bekerja di sini selama 5 tahun.


(-) I will not have been working here long.

Saya belum lama akan bekerja di sini.


(?) Will you have been working here for 5 years?

Apakah kamu akan sudah bekerja di sini selama 5 tahun?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

for 5 years, by next month, for a long time

2️⃣ Selalu Pakai WILL HAVE BEEN

Semua subject:

I/You/We/They/He/She → WILL HAVE BEEN

3️⃣ Verb + ING

work → working
study → studying
play → playing

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Semua subject pakai WILL HAVE BEEN

✅ Setelah BEEN harus verb + ING

✅ Dipakai untuk aksi yang berlangsung lama sampai masa depan

✅ Biasanya ada FOR + DURASI

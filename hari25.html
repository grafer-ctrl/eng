📖 SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

Digunakan untuk: kebiasaan, rutinitas, fakta umum, dan aktivitas sehari-hari.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + V1 (s/es) + O

(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) She reads a book every night.

Dia membaca buku setiap malam.


(-) She does not read a book every night.

Dia tidak membaca buku setiap malam.


(?) Does she read a book every night?

Apakah dia membaca buku setiap malam?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

always, usually, often, every day, sometimes, never

2️⃣ He / She / It

Tambahkan s/es pada kata kerja.

read → reads go → goes

3️⃣ Do / Does

I/You/We/They → DO

He/She/It → DOES

🧠 Cara Super Cepat Paham

✅ Kalimat positif = langsung pakai kata kerja

✅ He/She/It = verb tambah s/es

✅ Negatif = do/does + not + V1

✅ Pertanyaan = Do/Does di depan

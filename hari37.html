📖 PAST FUTURE TENSE

Digunakan untuk: rencana atau kejadian masa depan dari sudut pandang masa lalu.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + would + V1 + O

(-) S + would + not + V1 + O

(?) Would + S + V1 + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) He said he would come to the party.

Dia bilang dia akan datang ke pesta.


(-) He said he would not come to the party.

Dia bilang dia tidak akan datang ke pesta.


(?) Would he come to the party?

Apakah dia akan datang ke pesta?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

said, promised, thought, told

2️⃣ Selalu Pakai WOULD

Semua subject:

I/You/We/They/He/She → WOULD

3️⃣ Setelah WOULD = V1

would go
would eat
would study

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Semua subject pakai WOULD

✅ Setelah WOULD harus V1

✅ Dipakai untuk masa depan dari sudut pandang masa lalu

✅ Biasanya ada SAID atau TOLD

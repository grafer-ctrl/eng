📖 PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE

Digunakan untuk: aktivitas yang sedang terjadi sekarang dan rencana dekat.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + am/is/are + V-ing + O

(-) S + am/is/are + not + V-ing + O

(?) Am/Is/Are + S + V-ing + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) They are studying English now.

Mereka sedang belajar bahasa Inggris sekarang.


(-) They are not studying English now.

Mereka tidak sedang belajar bahasa Inggris sekarang.


(?) Are they studying English now?

Apakah mereka sedang belajar bahasa Inggris sekarang?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

now, right now, at the moment, currently, today

2️⃣ Gunakan TO BE

I → am
He/She/It → is
You/We/They → are

3️⃣ Verb + ING

play → playing
study → studying
read → reading

🧠 Cara Super Cepat Paham

✅ Selalu pakai am/is/are

✅ Kata kerja harus tambah ING

✅ Negatif = am/is/are + not

✅ Pertanyaan = Am/Is/Are di depan

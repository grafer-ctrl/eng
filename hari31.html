📖 PAST PERFECT TENSE

Digunakan untuk: kejadian yang telah selesai sebelum kejadian lain di masa lalu.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) The train had left before I arrived.

Kereta sudah pergi sebelum saya tiba.


(-) The train had not left before I arrived.

Kereta belum pergi sebelum saya tiba.


(?) Had the train left before you arrived?

Apakah kereta sudah pergi sebelum kamu tiba?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

before, after, when, already

2️⃣ Selalu Pakai HAD

Semua subject:

I/You/We/They/He/She → HAD

3️⃣ Gunakan V3

go → gone
eat → eaten
write → written

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Semua subject pakai HAD

✅ Setelah HAD harus V3

✅ Dipakai untuk kejadian yang lebih dulu selesai

✅ Biasanya ada BEFORE atau AFTER

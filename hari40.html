📖 PAST FUTURE PERFECT

Digunakan untuk: aksi yang akan sudah selesai dalam sudut pandang masa lalu.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + would + have + V3 + O

(-) S + would + not + have + V3 + O

(?) Would + S + have + V3 + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) She thought she would have finished by noon.

Dia pikir dia akan telah selesai sebelum siang.


(-) She thought she would not have finished by noon.

Dia pikir dia belum akan selesai sebelum siang.


(?) Would she have finished by noon?

Apakah dia akan telah selesai sebelum siang?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

thought, by noon, before, promised

2️⃣ Selalu Pakai WOULD HAVE

Semua subject:

I/You/We/They/He/She → WOULD HAVE

3️⃣ Gunakan V3

finish → finished
eat → eaten
write → written

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Semua subject pakai WOULD HAVE

✅ Setelah HAVE harus V3

✅ Dipakai untuk aksi yang sudah selesai versi masa lalu

✅ Biasanya ada BY + waktu

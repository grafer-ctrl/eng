📖 PAST FUTURE PERFECT CONTINUOUS

Digunakan untuk: aksi yang akan sudah berlangsung lama dalam sudut pandang masa lalu.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + would + have + been + V-ing + O

(-) S + would + not + have + been + V-ing + O

(?) Would + S + have + been + V-ing + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) He said he would have been waiting for 3 hours.

Dia bilang dia akan sudah menunggu selama 3 jam.


(-) He said he would not have been waiting long.

Dia bilang dia tidak akan lama menunggu.


(?) Would he have been waiting for 3 hours?

Apakah dia akan sudah menunggu selama 3 jam?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

for 3 hours, for years, since, long

2️⃣ Selalu Pakai WOULD HAVE BEEN

Semua subject:

I/You/We/They/He/She → WOULD HAVE BEEN

3️⃣ Verb + ING

wait → waiting
study → studying
work → working

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Semua subject pakai WOULD HAVE BEEN

✅ Setelah BEEN harus verb + ING

✅ Dipakai untuk aksi yang berlangsung lama versi masa lalu

✅ Biasanya ada FOR / SINCE

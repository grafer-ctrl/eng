📖 PAST CONTINUOUS TENSE

Digunakan untuk: aktivitas yang sedang terjadi di masa lalu.

📐 Rumus

(+) S + was/were + V-ing + O

(-) S + was/were + not + V-ing + O

(?) Was/Were + S + V-ing + O?

📝 Contoh

(+) I was sleeping when you called.

Saya sedang tidur ketika kamu menelepon.


(-) I was not sleeping when you called.

Saya tidak sedang tidur ketika kamu menelepon.


(?) Were you sleeping when I called?

Apakah kamu sedang tidur ketika saya menelepon?

🔥 Trik Cepat Hafal

1️⃣ Kata Kunci

when, while, at that time, last night

2️⃣ Gunakan WAS / WERE

I/He/She/It → WAS

You/We/They → WERE

3️⃣ Verb + ING

sleep → sleeping
study → studying
play → playing

🧠 Cara Cepat Paham

✅ Selalu pakai WAS atau WERE

✅ Kata kerja harus tambah ING

✅ Dipakai untuk aktivitas sedang berlangsung di masa lalu

✅ Sering dipakai dengan WHEN atau WHILE
